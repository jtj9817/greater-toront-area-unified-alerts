# Product Definition: GTA Alerts

## Vision
GTA Alerts aims to be the definitive, real-time intelligence hub for the Greater Toronto Area. By aggregating disparate emergency and transit data into a single, high-fidelity stream, we empower residents, commuters, and professionals with the situational awareness needed to navigate the region safely and efficiently.

## Target Audience
- **Residents and Commuters:** Individuals who require immediate, actionable information to make informed decisions about their safety, travel routes, and daily commutes in the face of unpredictable disruptions.
- **Emergency Service Personnel & Researchers:** Professionals and analysts who need to monitor regional incident trends, response patterns, and the overall pulse of emergency activity across the GTA for planning and study.
- **Open Data Enthusiasts & Developers:** Technical users who value the normalization of fragmented regional data and may use the platform as a reference for regional data aggregation best practices.

## Core Goals
- **Unified Low-Latency Dashboard:** To eliminate the need for checking multiple fragmented sources by providing a single, consolidated "source of truth" for fire, police, and transit alerts, updated in near real-time.
- **Historical Reporting & Trend Analysis:** Beyond immediate alerts, the platform will archive incident data to provide insights into regional patterns, helping users and researchers understand frequency and location trends of various alert types.

## MVP Features
- **Real-Time Unified Alert Feed:**
    - A centralized stream of alerts aggregated from multiple GTA sources.
    - Advanced filtering capabilities by category (e.g., Fire, Police, Transit) and data source (e.g., Toronto Fire CAD, TTC).
- **Detailed Incident Visualization:**
    - Dedicated views for individual incidents providing granular data such as event type, alarm levels, and unit assignments.
    - Integration of geographic location data to help users contextualize incidents relative to their position.
- **Searchable Historical Archive:**
    - A robust search interface allowing users to find specific past incidents.
    - Archival access to historical data for browsing and preliminary trend identification.

## Success Factors
- **Data Fidelity & Reliability:** The platform's value depends on its accuracy. Success is defined by mirroring official source data with minimal synchronization lag.
- **Accessibility & UX Excellence:** A modern, responsive interface that prioritizes speed and clarity, ensuring users can consume critical information quickly on any device.
- **Seamless Data Integration:** The successful technical normalization of diverse and often inconsistently formatted open data feeds from across the various municipalities and agencies in the GTA.
